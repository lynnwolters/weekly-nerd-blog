Project Presentations

May 22nd, 2024

Q42 is a digital agency known for their innovative products that enrich life, simplify tasks, and add enjoyment. Their diverse portfolio includes collaborations with leading brands such as HEMA, the Rijksmuseum, Philips Hue, Wie is de Mol, and Hack the Planet. Q-DNA is central to their commitment to accessibility, advocating for end users and fostering innovation.

Accessibility is a core value at Q42, where they conduct extensive testing with real users of diverse abilities. They focus on addressing the needs of individuals facing challenges related to hearing, vision, mobility, or understanding to ensure inclusivity in all their projects.

Axe DevTools, developed by Deque Systems, is a powerful accessibility testing extension for Google Chrome and Mozilla Firefox browsers. It is designed to seamlessly integrate into developers' workflows, enabling them to detect and address accessibility issues early through automated scans and detailed reports. In addition to identifying violations of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG), Axe DevTools provides practical remediation guidance, helping developers understand best practices for accessibility.

Sense Math was a groundbreaking initiative by Q42 aimed at assisting blind students in interpreting mathematical graphs. Leveraging innovative technology, they developed an app that translates visual data into auditory cues. This significantly reduces the time students need to understand complex graphs, making mathematics more accessible and intuitive.

Appt.org serves as a platform empowering developers to create more accessible websites. It offers comprehensive resources on integrating accessibility features across various platforms, including iOS and Android. Appt.org also provides official guidelines and code documentation to promote inclusive design practices.

Oculi Mundi, meaning 'Eyes of the World', is a digital heritage site housing the Sunderland Collection. This collection includes world maps, celestial maps, atlases, globes, and knowledge books, curated with a deep passion for travel, history, and imagination. Q42's challenge was to ensure the website's accessibility without compromising its aesthetic appeal, striking a balance between inclusivity and visual refinement.
