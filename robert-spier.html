Life After CMD

May 15th, 2024

Robert Spier completed his education in Communication & Multimedia Design (CMD) and subsequently pursued Information Systems at the University of Amsterdam. While he worked as a lecturer in CMD, he transitioned to a role as a creative technologist at Deloitte. During his presentation, Robert shared his experiences and advice on potential careers after CMD, including insights into potential earnings.

What does a Creative Technologist do? Robert describes himself as a digital superhero involved in front-end development, emerging technologies, qualitative research, stakeholder management, devising creative solutions, and automation. He primarily works on internal projects at Deloitte, such as developing a chatGPT for internal use and creating a deepfake of the company's founder for internal announcements.

Career Opportunities after CMD After CMD, there are diverse career paths available, including user experience design, front-end development, and even marketing (if combined with a minor in entrepreneurship). Within development, numerous roles are possible, ranging from working at design agencies and webshops to freelancing or further academic study.

Examples and Salaries Robert shared some examples of recent graduates' achievements, including the tools they used and their salaries:

* Secondment at an energy company in 2022 with Angular and Tailwind, salary € 3000,- * Traineeship in the financial sector in 2023 with Vue and Bootstrap, salary € 2900,- * Freelance work for a design studio in 2017 with React/Next and Sanity, salary € 2100,- * Internship at a full-service agency in 2023 with Svelte and Vite, salary € 2600,-

5 Tips for Job Hunting Robert emphasized five key points for finding a job after CMD:

* Explore sectors: Research various sectors and corporate cultures. * Type of employer: Consider freelance, secondment, or permanent positions and the technology stacks they use. * Salary expectations: Discuss and research salaries and living costs. * Benefits: Consider additional perks such as travel reimbursement, pension plans, and bonuses. * Work environment: Ensure a good match in terms of company culture and projects.
